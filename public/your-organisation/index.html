For Teams

How Clue House fits into your team, workplace, studio, or organisation.

Clue House is designed to do the heavy lifting.
We create, host, and guide a polished puzzle experience that fits smoothly around your people — without adding work to your team.

No accounts, sign-ups or installs. Your people don’t create profiles, passwords, or download anything.
They arrive, play, and leave.
100% ad and pop-up free. No distractions pretending to be gameplay. Just the game, exactly as intended.
Automatic opt-out. Anyone who doesn’t engage simply stops receiving game emails.
No forms. No friction. No awkward follow-ups.

You choose the game; we handle the rest.

Whether you’re a tech team, creative studio, startup, council, nonprofit, or workplace looking for something different, Clue House gives your people a shared experience to talk about, return to, and solve together.

We help you launch the experience cleanly, with simple communication, clear timing, and a friendly heads-up for anyone who needs to know what is going out.

Our invitations are intriguing without being intrusive — a light touch of mystery, and the sense that something is already underway: the first clue is waiting. The moment someone clicks, they’re transported straight into the game, and the adventure begins. They can bookmark the page and return whenever it suits them.

For players who enjoy the mystery but forget the calendar, optional clue alerts keep the story moving.

Along the way, lifelines can help those who need a nudge, while the leaderboard gives the competitive souls somewhere to shine.

Anyone who isn’t interested doesn’t need to do anything. They simply stop receiving game emails, while those who want to play keep following the trail.

Too good to be true?
No — that’s just how we treat guests in our house.
