About Clue House

A small look behind the puzzles — the origins, design choices, and quiet values beneath each clue.

Clue House is an independent puzzle studio based in Tauranga, New Zealand, crafting intelligent puzzle experiences that spark curiosity and bring people together. Every experience is built from first clue to final reveal, blending narrative intrigue, fair challenge, and seamless delivery for teams, workplaces, and organisations.

It exists to bring extraordinary moments into ordinary places — experiences that arrive with anticipation and stay with you long after they’re solved.

The games are intentionally low-tech, story-led, and human. Each clue is made to be worked through — turned over, tested, and understood — before anything truly resolves.

What matters here
  • 🧠 Puzzles that are fair, kind and clever.
  • 🕊️ Experiences that fit around real life, not over it.
  • 🔐 Respect for privacy, data and attention.
  • 🤝 Clarity: you always know what you’re getting.
