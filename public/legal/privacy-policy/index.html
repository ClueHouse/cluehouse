Privacy Policy

If you’re on this page, it means you take this stuff seriously. We do too.
Clue House wants you to know exactly how your info is used — no tricks, no fluff.
We only collect what we need, use it how we said we would, and ditch it when we’re done.
What we collect:
Just your email.
Why we use it:
To send clues, updates, and invite you to future games†.
Where we keep it:
Securely in KIT. Never copied, sold, or shared.
How long we keep it:
Until the game ends — then we delete it‡.
Your rights:
Unsubscribe anytime by emailing opt@cluehouse.co.nz.
No tracking:
No cookies, no behaviour data. Just puzzles.
Global compliance:
GDPR, COPPA, and we don’t collect info from under-13s.
† If you opted in when asked earlier.
‡ Unless you said yes to future invites — then we keep it until asked to remove it.

FOR THE LEGAL NERDS

(You read privacy policies for fun? We respect that.)

We collect only your email address, and only if you opt in via KIT (Keep In Touch) to receive Clue House updates.

We don’t collect names, phone numbers, or personal identifiers.

Consent is under GDPR Article 6(1)(a).

We use your email to send clues, track participation, and notify you of future games (if opted in).

We do not send marketing emails, sell your data, or share it with third parties.

Your email is stored securely via KIT using privacy-compliant providers.

We do not export or manually download your data.

Clue House is the Data Controller; external systems act confidentially as Processors.

Your email is deleted within 30 days after each game unless you opted in for future invites.

If you opted in, we retain it until you opt out.

You can access, correct, or delete your data anytime.

Email fix@cluehouse.co.nz for fast fixes.

Or unsubscribe using any KIT email link.

We use no cookies, analytics, trackers, or behavioural monitoring.

We don’t track clicks, time on page, or anything else you do here.

If the policy changes, we update this page and notify you as needed.

We operate from New Zealand and the UK following GDPR, COPPA & NZ law.

Our services are for users aged 13 and above.

If we ever receive info from someone under 13, we delete it immediately.

We treat your trust seriously and use only what’s needed to deliver the games you came to play.
